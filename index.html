  • Yet Another Continuous Extension of the Collatz Function

    I have found a continuous extension of the Collatz function which seems to have no cycle besides (1;2) and its fixed points, and which converges to 1 even from the close vicinity of its positive real fixed points, usually with lower stopping times than other continuous extensions. I have rendered a few nice visualizations of it on the complex plane.

    Open
