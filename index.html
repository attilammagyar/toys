Toys

Pet projects that are too small to be worth their own separate repositories and to be developed and maintained very rigorously.

  • Chimp Memory Game
    Memorize the randomly scattered numbers, then click or touch them in ascending order - if you can.
  • Kanji Practice
    A writing based, minimalistic flashcard (SRS, Spaced Repetition System) app for learning Japanese kanji.
  • Flashcards
    A minimalistic flashcard (SRS, Spaced Repetition System) app for language learning and more, with a dark color scheme.
  • Calcle
    A game where you have to assemble the longest correct calculation with the highest result you can. New challenge every day. (Inspired by Wordle.)
  • Image to WAV
    Turn an image into sound: this image synth toy can generate a sound which reproduces a picture when you look at its spectrogram.
